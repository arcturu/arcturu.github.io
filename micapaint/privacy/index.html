Privacy Policy

[English] [日本語]

This app uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics provided by Google, as well as crash reports provided by Apple, to improve stability and optimize user experience. The information collected does not directly identify individuals and is processed statistically. Additionally, the information is NEVER provided to third parties except for Google and Apple.

Information Collected

  • Time required for app launch, file opening, brush drawing, etc.
  • General environment information such as device type and OS version
  • Event information regarding screen transitions and feature usage
  • Crash reports

Information that can identify individuals or the content of works in progress is NEVER collected.

Changes

This privacy policy may be changed as necessary. If there are important changes, they will be announced on the official website.
